Tatry Mountains Holiday 2006

Magda and I went to the Tatry Mountains in June 2006. They are on the border of Slovakia and Poland. Normally there would not be snow in June!. This made the walks we planned a lot impossible and meant we had to rush out and buy crampons and special boots before travelling.

We went with Jenny and Paul.
