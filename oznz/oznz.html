Australia and New Zealand Trip
From March to July 2005 I backpacked around Australia and New Zealand. I travelled to lots of places and took lots of photos. On this page I give a brief summary of my itinerary and links to some of the best photos which I took.
Brief Itinerary
Perth and around- 1 week
Adelaide- 1 week
Tasmania- 10 days
Melbourne- 1 week
New Zealand South Island- 5.5 weeks
New Zealand North Island- 2.5 weeks
Sydney- 1 week
East Coast of OZ- 5 weeks
